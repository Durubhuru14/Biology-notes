IMP Definations

Inheritance or Heredity:The transmission of genetic materials from older generation to next generation is called "Inheritance" or "heredity"

Genetics:It is the branch of biology with deals with study of "Heredity" and "Variations".

Cytogenetics:The study of inheritance in relation to the structure and function of chromosomes is called Cytogenetics.

Microbiology:Microbiology is the study of the biology of microscopic organisms - viruses, bacteria, algae, fungi, slime molds, and protozoa.
